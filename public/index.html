HPS.

Patients and deceased's Information all centralized.

HPS is the best way to organize, manage and render hospital patients's personal information, medication history and appointments to the doctors, admins as well as forensic scientist - it is designed to solve the slow services, and the easy loss of data that has been there for years.

About

Different Hospitals, One Application.

HPS assist in keeping the department of health records much more easier and doctors together with patients not having to go an extra mile. We align with doctors in keeping track of their patients of the different help they have receieved as shown in the patient database.

When a patient is deceased, the doctors can notice a change in their records as it will report more from the forensic side. As HPS we saw a serious problem of patients forgetting their checkup dates and fetching their medication, so a call is made to them as a reminder and also medication deliveries.
